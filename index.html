Books Categories

A broad term referring to general aspects of the book's content and audience

Textbooks

Testbook is an Indian educational technology company, headquartered in Mumbai, India. Testbook prepares students for various competitive exams such as GATE, State PSC, SBI PO, IBPS PO, UPSC IAS Exam, Engineering Recruitment Exams, and SSC Exams.

Tamil

The term "Tamil book" can refer to a book that is written in the Tamil language or a book that explores Tamil literature, culture, history, or any other aspect related to the Tamil-speaking people.

Science

A science book typically refers to a book that explores and explains concepts, theories, or discoveries related to the natural or physical world. These books can cover a wide range of scientific disciplines, including physics, chemistry, biology, astronomy, and more.

History

These books can cover a wide range of historical periods, from ancient civilizations to recent decades, and they come in various formats, including textbooks, academic works, popular histories, and biographies.

Biology

Biology books are written works that delve into the study of living organisms and their interactions with each other and their environments.

Story

Storybooks are literary works that tell a narrative or fictional story. They can come in various genres and formats, catering to different age groups and preferences.
